Finnvedens Bil

Home » Holdings » Finnvedens Bil

Finnvedens Bil is a dealer of Volvo and Dacia cars, Renault cars and light transport vehicles. Of all new car sales in Värnamo and its surroundings, nearly one out of five cars is sold by Finnvedens Bil. The company also has customers throughout Sweden through the used car trade on the Internet. The customer offering also includes full-service and body-repair shops, sales of parts and fuel, tyre storage and  car hire operations.
