LWW Group

Home » Holdings » LWW Group

LWW Group is currently the second largest manufacturer of copper and aluminium winding wire for electrical motors, generators and transformers in Europe. The Group has one of Europe’s most modern plants in its area and is the industry leader in environmental issues.

The customers are major energy and infrastructure companies, as well as manufactures of consumer products such as white goods and cars.
