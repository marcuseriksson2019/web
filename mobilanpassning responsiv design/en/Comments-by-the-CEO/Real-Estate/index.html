Own Properties Strengthen Business Development

Functional properties are important strategic resource in the development of Liljedahl Group’s operations. Liljedahl Real Estate is responsible for optimizing the Group’s property portfolio.

Most of the properties utilized for Group operations are owned by the Group. As the owner, Liljedahl Group is able to efficiently and flexibly adapt the properties to its needs, both in the short and long term.

Through the Real Estate Division, the Group owns, manages and develops 19 properties within several countries, with a total floor space of 180,000 square meters. The premises are leased at market-competitive rents.
