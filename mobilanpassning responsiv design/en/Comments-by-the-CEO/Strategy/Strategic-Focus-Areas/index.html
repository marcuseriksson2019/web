Strategic Focus Areas

Home » Liljedahl Group » Strategy » Strategic Focus Areas

1. Profitable growth

Liljedahl Group’s strategy is growth while observing the financial objectives and with a focus on market share. Growth will primarily take place organically, but may also be generated through supplemental acquisitions within current business areas.

2. Product and service development

Business area Industry is active in mature industries. The development of current and new product segments is important for growth and increased profitability. Considerable growth potential can also be found in supplemental business concerning services in the commercial business area.

3. Process efficiency

Increased competition and the market’s constant demand for lower costs require continual improvement of the companies’ value-generating processes.
