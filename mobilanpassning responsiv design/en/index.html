Welcome to The Liljedahl Group

Liljedahl Group is a family-owned group of companies with seven wholly-owned holdings: Elcowire Group, LWW Group, Hörle Wire Group, LMT Group, Finnvedens Lastvagnar, Finnvedens Bil and Liljedahl Group Real Estate. Liljedahl Group is also the largest owner of the listed company Bufab AB.
